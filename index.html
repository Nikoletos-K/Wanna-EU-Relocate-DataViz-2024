Wanna EU Relocate?

Are you thinking about moving to Europe?

Whether you’re a digital nomad, an expat, or simply looking for a fresh start, "Wanna EU Relocate?" is here to help you make an informed decision. We provide you with comprehensive dashboards, having all latest information (2023), that cover some of the most crucial aspects of living in different European countries. Here’s a brief overview of what you’ll find on our site:

Criterion 1: Citizen Satisfaction Index

One of the key factors in deciding where to relocate is understanding how satisfied the current residents are with their country. Our Citizen Satisfaction Index offers you an insightful look into the happiness and satisfaction levels across various European nations. This index takes into account factors like quality of life, access to services, and overall well-being.

  • Happiness and well-being scores
  • Access to healthcare and education
  • Quality of public services

Criterion 2: Tax and Net Income Proportions

Understanding the financial implications of relocating is crucial. Our dashboard on average tax proportions versus net income helps you get a clear picture of what you can expect to earn after taxes. This allows you to plan your finances better and choose a country that aligns with your financial goals.

  • Average tax rates
  • Net income comparisons
  • Country-specific tax benefits

Criterion 3: Internet Speed

For digital nomads and remote workers, internet speed is non-negotiable. Our internet speed dashboard provides you with up-to-date information on the average internet speeds across European countries. Stay connected and productive no matter where you decide to settle.

  • Average download and upload speeds
  • Broadband coverage
  • Mobile internet availability

Criterion 4: Living Costs Comparison

Cost of living can vary greatly from one country to another. Our living costs comparison dashboard gives you a detailed breakdown of expenses like rent, groceries, and overall purchasing power. This helps you understand what your daily life might cost in different parts of Europe.

  • Rent and housing costs
  • Grocery and utility expenses
  • Overall purchasing power

A Note to Digital Nomads

As a digital nomad, your lifestyle requires flexibility, good connectivity, and a conducive environment to work and live. Our dashboards are designed with you in mind, offering critical insights to help you find the perfect European destination that meets your professional and personal needs. Whether it's a vibrant city with fast internet or a peaceful town with a low cost of living, we’ve got you covered.

Get Started

Dive into our dashboards and start exploring the various facets of life in Europe. "Wanna EU Relocate?" is your go-to resource for making a well-informed decision on your next big move. Happy exploring, and we hope you find your perfect European home!
